About This Visualization

This visualization helps you grasp large numbers by representing people in stadium-sized units. Each stadium holds 100,000 people.

The 3D view allows you to explore the stadiums from any angle, giving a better sense of scale for large populations.

The actual count is always accurate in the information panel above.
